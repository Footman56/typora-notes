1. 选择启动文件

   配置启动参数

   -Dspring.config.location=classpath:application-local119.properties -Ddubbo.resolve.file=/Users/peilizhi/javaProjects/xrxs-appraisal-springboot/appraisal-webfront/target/classes/dubbo-direct.properties

2. 取消pom中的配置

   ```xml
   <!--        <resources>-->
   <!--            <resource>-->
   <!--                <directory>src/main/resources</directory>-->
   <!--                <excludes>-->
   <!--                    <exclude>application*.properties</exclude>-->
   <!--                    <exclude>log4j2/log4j2-*.xml</exclude>-->
   <!--                </excludes>-->
   <!--                <includes>-->
   <!--                    <include>**/*</include>-->
   <!--                </includes>-->
   <!--            </resource>-->
   <!--        </resources>-->
   ```
